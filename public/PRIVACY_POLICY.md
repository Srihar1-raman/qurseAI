## 4. Data Storage and Retention

### 4.1 Database Storage

**We use Supabase as our database provider.** Your data is stored in our database, including:
- Account information and preferences
- Conversations and messages
- Billing and subscription status
- Usage tracking and rate limit enforcement
- File attachment metadata and references

**File Storage:**
- **Cloud Storage Providers:** Files are stored using cloud storage services
- **Providers may include:** Supabase Storage, AWS S3, Cloudflare R2, Google Cloud Storage, or others
- **Storage locations:** Files may be stored in different regions/countries than our database
- **Encryption:** File storage may be encrypted at rest and/or in transit
- **Provider changes:** We may change storage providers without notice

### 4.2 Data Retention Periods

**Active Accounts:**
- **Account Data:** Retained indefinitely while your account is active
- **Conversations:** Retained until you delete them or your account
- **Messages:** Retained until you delete them or your account
- **File Attachments:** Retained until you delete the associated conversation/message, or until we delete files after a certain period
- **Activity Data:** Retained for service optimization

**Guest Users:**
- **Temporary Storage:** Guest conversations are stored temporarily
- **Automatic Cleanup:** Guest data is deleted periodically via automated cleanup job
- **No Long-term Retention:** Guest data is not permanently stored

**Deleted Accounts:**
- **Immediate Deletion:** All Supabase data is deleted immediately upon account deletion
  - User profile
  - Conversations and messages
  - Preferences
  - Rate limit records
  - Authentication data
- **File Deletion:** We attempt to delete all uploaded files from cloud storage
  - Some files may remain due to storage provider limitations, backup retention, or technical constraints
  - Files already sent to AI model providers may remain in their systems
  - We cannot guarantee complete or immediate file deletion
- **Data Retained by Third-Party Services:**
  - **Vercel Analytics:** May retain page views, performance metrics, and device/browser information per their data retention policy
  - **Sentry:** May retain error reports and crash logs for debugging and monitoring per their data retention policy

### 4.3 Third-Party Data Retention
