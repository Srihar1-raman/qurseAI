## 1. Introduction

Qurse ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, secure, and improve our services. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

**Important:** We do not sell your personal data. We use cookies only to provide and improve our service.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

**Cookies are small text files** that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. Cookies serve various purposes:

- **Remembering your preferences**
- **Keeping you signed in**
- **Understanding how you use the service**
- **Improving your experience**

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

**These cookies are essential for the Service to work.** Without them, the Service cannot function.

**What they do:**
- **Authentication:** Keep you signed in to your account
- **Session Management:** Remember you as you move between pages
- **Security:** Prevent fraud and protect your account
- **Rate Limiting:** Track usage to prevent abuse

**How long they last:** Only as long as needed for the feature to work

**Opt-out:** Not available - these are required for the Service to work

### 3.2 Performance and Functionality Cookies

**These cookies remember your choices and preferences.**

**What they remember:**
- **Theme:** Your choice of light mode, dark mode, or auto
- **AI Model:** Which AI model you prefer to use
- **Settings:** Any customizations you make
- **Language:** Your language preferences

**How long they last:** Long-term

**Opt-out:** Yes, but this may reset your preferences to default

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies

**These cookies help us understand how people use the Service.**

**What they track:**
- **Usage Statistics:** How many messages are sent
- **Popular Features:** Which AI models and features are used most
- **Error Tracking:** Helps us identify and fix bugs
- **Performance:** Ensures the Service is fast and reliable

**How long they last:** Varies according to provider policies

**Opt-out:** Yes (see "Your Browser Controls" section below)

### 3.4 Third-Party Cookies

**Some cookies are set by external services we use to power Qurse:**

**Performance Monitoring (Vercel):**
- **What they track:** Page views, how long you stay on the site, website speed
- **Purpose:** Helps us improve performance and fix issues
- **How long:** Varies according to Vercel's policy
- **Privacy Policy:** [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

**Login and Security (Supabase):**
- **What they track:** Your login session, security information
- **Purpose:** Keeps you signed in and protects your account

**Payment Processing (Dodo Payments):**
- **What they track:** Checkout session, security data
- **Purpose:** Processes your subscription payments safely

**AI Providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, XAI, Groq, etc.):**
- **What they might track:** Their own cookies for their services
- **Purpose:** Power the AI chat features

**Duration:** Varies by service

**Opt-out:** Varies by service (see "Your Browser Controls" section)

## 7. Your Browser Controls

### 7.1 Managing Cookies

**Most web browsers allow you to:**
- **View** what cookies are stored
- **Delete** existing cookies
- **Block** cookies from specific websites
- **Block** third-party cookies
- **Block** all cookies
- **Clear** cookies when you close the browser

**How to access browser settings:**
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage cookies

### 7.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

**If you disable or block cookies:**
- **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** The Service will not function. You will not be able to:
  - Sign in to your account
  - Maintain your session
  - Use the chat features
  - Make payments

- **Functionality Cookies:** Your preferences will not be saved:
  - Theme selection will reset
  - Settings will not persist
  - Customizations will be lost

- **Analytics Cookies:** This will not affect functionality but will:
  - Limit our ability to improve the Service
  - Reduce our ability to fix bugs
  - Impact our understanding of usage patterns

**We recommend keeping strictly necessary cookies enabled for the best experience.**
